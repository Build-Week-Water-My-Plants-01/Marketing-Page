Water My Plants

An easy way to keep track of your plant's watering needs.


It can be difficult to keep track of your plants watering needs, especially as your collection grows.
Water My Plants is a free and easy to use app that helps you effectively manage a busy garden.

With

you can:

Set reminders for watering

Set reminders for feeding

Keep track of your plant collection
